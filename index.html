About Aquavita

Freshly renovated, this stunning two-story beachfront home features four spacious, well-lit bedrooms and two vibrant bathrooms, all tastefully decorated.

The upper level is adorned with sliding glass doors that lead to an expansive wrap-around balcony equipped with a luxury hammock and a high-end stainless steel BBQ for gourmet outdoor dining.

Enjoy the sounds of the ocean waves with a drink in hand, or take a short walk to the glorious Teewah Beach for swimming, surfing, fishing, or simply relaxing. From the moment you arrive, excitement is just steps away from your front door.

Aquavita, the first house you encounter upon entering Teewah, is a place you'll find hard to leave.
