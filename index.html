About Ivy Mae Photography

Ivy Mae Photography is the new photography business of Ivy Chapel. Ivy recently graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Photography and Illustration, but her style speaks volumes to her experience. Ivy has worked on as part of a number of freelance projects, and is looking to work on more professional projects in the fashion and editorial photography business, with a keen interest in portraiture.
